FLORAL Bouquet

FLORAL Bouquet is a type of seasonal flower subscription where you receive a beautiful bouquet of flowers once a week for the duration of selected flower season.

The 2023 FLORAL Bouquet options:

May Flower Season – $125.00

A premium bunch of unique, early spring flowers every week during the month of May: Ranunculus, anemone, specialty narcissus, tulips, icelandic poppies, snapdragon, bupleurum, cress or honeysuckle foliage.

June Flower Season – $140.00

A premium mixed bouquet of late spring flowers every week during the month of June: Peonies, campanula, snapdragons, stock, bupleurum, baptisia, cress, yarrow, delphinium, echinacea, astilbe, feverfew, lupine, scabiosa or foxglove.

July or August Flower Season – $125.00

A premium bunch of unique, summer flowers every week during the month of July or August: Zinnia, rudbeckia, lisianthus, dahlia, veronica, ageratum, dara, snapdragon, dianthus, sunflower, echinacea, mountain mint, gladiolus, cosmos, celosia or ecualyptus.

September Flower Season – $125.00

A premium bunch of unique, fall flowers every week during the month of September: Dahlia, lisianthus, eucalyptus, zinnia, celosia, rudbeckia, ageratum, amaranthus or strawflower.

Each bunch comes with about 8 – 10 stems of hand-selected blooms. As the season approaches you will be weekly reminded via email about pick-up times and dates.

Through our FLORAL Bouquet subscription, you will have access to unbelievable quality cut flowers that you can’t find anywhere else. Our FLORAL Bouquet makes a unique gift for anyone.

To get your FLORAL Bouquet send us an email!

Farmers Market

The Yaremche Farmers Market functions to support small, family run farms and their families, participate in local agriculture, help preserve and protect farmland and open space, and in general support local economies and small business.

We have participated at the beautiful Sunday morning farmer’s market for the past three seasons, and we look forward to being back next April for the 2023 Season!

Every week, you'll have a chance to meet directly with the farmers and enjoy our seasonal flower bouquets.

The Yaremche Farmers Market

April through October
Every Sunday, 8 am – 1 pm, rain or shine!

Lisova Farm
41 Svobody Street, Yaremche, IF 78500
