About us

FLORAL was founded in 2015 and is based in Ivano-Frankivsk, Ukraine.

It was named with the hope that happiness will be born through flowers and plants in this place.

Set in the middle of beautiful Ukrainian Carpathian Mountains, our fields are bursting with countless flower varieties. Our shop features our very favorite local, seasonal flowers and grasses. And our diverse small team aspires to making other peopleâ€™s lives more beautiful with these flowers and plants.

We care deeply about the environment and are committed to conducting our business in harmony with nature.

We source flowers and plants from local specialty flower farmers that practice sustainable farming, drying, and processing techniques. Our team, too, integrates sustainability into every decision we make in our retail store and online business.

We believe in sharing. Our business donates 5% of revenue to support the mission of local nonprofit organizations.

We are so fortunate to have the opportunity to spend our days working with other like-minded businesses that choose to support fellow farmers, women entrepreneurs, and the creative community. We cherish every opportunity to partner with small and upcoming vendors, designers, artisans and photographers.

FLORAL has been featured in FLOWER, HOUSE&GARDEN, Vogue, Martha Stewart Living, The English Garden, Florists' Review, let it bloom., Magnolia Journal, in her studio, and many other international magazines.

A small bouquet of flowers has the power to stir emotions, transport you back in time to happy memories and connect two perfect strangers.

There is such a wide variety of flowers and plants out there, and the possibilities are endless.
